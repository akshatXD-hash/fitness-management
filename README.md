## 📖 About

**FitMyLife** is a web application that helps you take control of your health — all from one place. Instead of juggling separate apps for workouts, diet, sleep, and body metrics, FitMyLife brings everything together into a single personalized dashboard.

Whether you're trying to lose weight, build muscle, sleep better, or simply stay consistent — FitMyLife gives you the tools to track your journey and make smarter health decisions.

> Track. Analyze. Improve. — All in one place.

---

## ✨ What You Can Do

### 🔐 Secure Account
Create your personal account and keep all your health data private, accessible only to you.

### 👤 Personal Profile
Set up your profile with details like age, gender, height, weight, and fitness goals. The app uses this to personalize your experience and calculations.

### 🏃 Workout Planner
Build a custom workout routine based on your current fitness level, target weight, preferred workout type, and how many days a week you want to train.

### 🥗 Diet Plans
Browse and manage diet strategies that complement your fitness goals — whether it's weight loss, muscle gain, or just eating healthier.

### 😴 Sleep Tracker
Log your sleep every day along with notes on how you felt. See your sleep patterns over time and understand how rest impacts your performance.

### 📊 BMI Tracker
Enter your height and weight to instantly calculate your BMI. Every entry is saved with a date so you can watch your progress over time.

### 🖥️ Health Dashboard
A clean, centralized dashboard that shows your BMI records, workout plans, diet info, and sleep logs — all at a glance. No more switching between screens.

### 📈 Progress Visualization
View your health history through charts and graphs. Spot trends, celebrate milestones, and stay motivated to keep going.
